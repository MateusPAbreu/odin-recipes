Salmagundi

Description

Salmagundi (alternatively salmagundy or sallid magundi) is a cold dish or salad made from different ingredients which may include meat, seafood, eggs, cooked and raw vegetables, fruits, or pickles. In English culture, the term does not refer to a single recipe but describes the grand presentation of a large plated salad of many disparate ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce or cabbage chopped
  • Boiled egg sliced
  • Sardines or other preserved fish
  • Onion thinly sliced
  • Pickled cucumber and carrots
  • Olives
  • Mango diced (optional, based on availability)
  • Grilled chicken shredded

Cooking instructions

  1. Arrange the lettuce or cabbage as the base on a large plate or bowl.
  2. Layer the boiled egg, sardines, onion, pickled cucumber, carrots, olives, mango, and grilled chicken on top.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, mustard seed, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss lightly.
  5. And it is ready to be served.
